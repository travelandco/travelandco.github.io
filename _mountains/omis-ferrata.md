### The Iron Way to the Fortress

The Fortica Via Ferrata in Omiš offers an exhilarating protected climbing route to the Starigrad (Fortica) Fortress. It provides stunning views of the town of Omiš, the Cetina River canyon, the river delta, and the sparkling Adriatic Sea with its islands.

### Route Details

This route is considered **beginner-friendly** and is an excellent introduction to via ferrata climbing, requiring no previous experience but a basic level of fitness and a head for heights.

* **Difficulty Grade:** B (Easy to Moderate).
* **Length/Duration:** Approximately **1.2 km** of ferrata route, with a total time of **1.5 to 2.5 hours** (climb and descent).
* **Equipment:** A standard via ferrata set, helmet, and proper hiking shoes are required.
* **Highlights:** The climb is secured with steel cables and steps and culminates at the 15th-century Fortica Fortress, a historic landmark with panoramic vistas.
