Trebević rises directly above Sarajevo, serving as the city's backyard wilderness and a powerful symbol of resilience, bearing witness to both Olympic glory and wartime struggle.

## Overview

The closest mountain to Sarajevo's center, Trebević offers quick escape into nature with stunning views over the entire city and valley. The recently renovated cable car makes the summit area accessible to everyone.

## Historical Significance

### Olympic Heritage
Trebević hosted bobsled and luge events during the 1984 Winter Olympics. The abandoned bobsled track has become an iconic landmark and poignant reminder of the mountain's complex history.

### Recent History
During the 1992-1995 siege, Trebević held strategic importance. Today, the mountain symbolizes Sarajevo's resilience and renewal, with nature reclaiming war-torn areas.

## Key Features

### Cable Car
The renovated Trebević cable car (originally built for the Olympics) offers spectacular views and easy summit access, making the mountain accessible to all visitors.

### Bobsled Track
The hauntingly beautiful abandoned Olympic bobsled track has become one of Sarajevo's most photographed locations, covered in graffiti art and reclaimed by nature.

### Pine Forests
Dense pine forests cover the mountain's slopes, offering pleasant walking and mountain biking through shaded trails.

## Recommended Routes

### 1. Cable Car Summit Walk
- **Duration:** 2-3 hours
- **Difficulty:** Easy
- **Starting point:** Cable car top station
- **Highlights:** Panoramic views, restaurants, easy walking

### 2. Bobsled Track Exploration
- **Duration:** 1-2 hours
- **Difficulty:** Easy
- **Highlights:** Olympic history, unique photo opportunities, street art

### 3. Full Mountain Traverse
- **Duration:** 5-6 hours
- **Difficulty:** Moderate
- **Highlights:** Complete mountain crossing, multiple viewpoints, forest trails

### 4. Hiking from the City
- **Duration:** 3-4 hours to summit
- **Difficulty:** Moderate
- **Starting point:** Various city trails
- **Highlights:** Workout hike, free access, city escape

## Practical Information

- **Access:** Cable car from Sarajevo (15 minutes) or hiking trails from various city points
- **Accommodation:** Hotels in Sarajevo, mountain restaurant at top station
- **Facilities:** Restaurant, café at cable car station
- **Water:** Available at facilities
- **Permits:** None required

## Best Time to Visit

Year-round destination. Summer offers pleasant hiking and biking. Autumn brings beautiful foliage. Winter provides easy snowshoe walks. Spring features wildflowers.

## Activities

- Hiking and walking
- Mountain biking (popular trail network)
- Cable car sightseeing
- Photography (bobsled track, city views)
- Paragliding (launch sites available)

## Safety Notes

- Very safe and accessible
- Good mobile coverage throughout
- Well-maintained trails
- Close to city - easy emergency access
- Be mindful of remaining mine warnings in off-trail areas (well-marked)

## Getting There

- Cable car: From Sarajevo city center
- Hiking: Multiple trailheads throughout the city
- Taxi: To various starting points
- Mountain biking: Direct trails from the city
