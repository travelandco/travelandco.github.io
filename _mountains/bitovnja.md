Bitovnja acts as a natural border between central Bosnia and Herzegovina, characterized by its accessibility and gentle yet impressive slopes.

## Overview

Bitovnja is a vast mountain plateau with several peaks, rich in water sources and blueberry bushes. It is perfect for long ridge walks and offers spectacular views during the golden hour.
