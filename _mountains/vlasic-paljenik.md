### The Heart of Central Bosnia

Vlašić is a prominent mountain and vast plateau located in the geographical center of Bosnia and Herzegovina, renowned for its extensive pastures, cattle-breeding tradition, and the famous **Vlašić cheese**. The plateau itself sits at an average altitude of approximately 1,300 meters.

The mountain's highest point is **Paljenik** (also referred to as Opaljenik), standing at **1,943 meters** above sea level. The nearest town is Travnik, which the mountain overlooks.

### Activity Information

Vlašić is a major center for both winter and summer tourism, with Babanovac being the most popular resort area.

* **Highest Peak:** Paljenik (1,943m).
* **Accessibility:** The summit is considered an **easy, technically undemanding** hike from areas like Babanovac or the Devečani mountain hut.
* **Hiking Routes:** Numerous well-marked routes are available, often connecting to the **Via Dinarica Green Trail**.

* **Tourism:** Vlašić has status as an air spa *(zračna banja)* due to its beneficial climate, making it popular for year-round activities like hiking, mountain biking, and skiing.
