About Us

Travel&Co is an association of passionate hikers dedicated to exploring and documenting the beauty of Balkan nature. Our mission is to connect people through a shared love of the outdoors, hiking, and adventure.

Through our expeditions across Bosnia and Herzegovina, Croatia, and Montenegro, we create unforgettable experiences and document pristine natural landscapes for future generations. Every ascent, every trail, and every summit carries a unique story that we share with our community.

We are currently working on the formal establishment of the association and preparing all necessary documents for official registration.
